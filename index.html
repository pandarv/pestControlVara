Pest Treatments

Fly

Musca Domestica, the common housefly, is widespread in all regions around the world where there have been human towns and villages. It reproduces in large numbers in animal excrement and rubbish, then migrates to human homes, becoming a nuisance.

Bedbug

Bed bugs usually reside in public places such as theatres, transportation, hospitals, hotels, and hostels, among many other places. People's belongings and garments are the primary careers to spread from source to destination. Bed bugs conceal themselves in comparatively tiny holes and cracks during the day and suck on human blood nighttime.

Rodent

Rodents are very clever pests that routinely relocate from one location to another. As bait phobia is a particularly distinctive feature in rodents, they can predict their death. Rodents play a significant role in propagating public illnesses such as plague, leptospirosis, and other diseases to people and other animals.
