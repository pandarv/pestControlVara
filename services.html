Bedbug Control Treatment

Bed bugs are hitchhikers and can be found in public transportation, hospitals, theatres, hotels, and hostels, among other places. They go from place to place with people's belongings and apparel. Bed bugs hide in small cracks and crevices during the day and feast on human blood at night. They're called "Bed Bugs" since they're primarily found in beds. Bed bugs prefer to travel in groups of two or three.

Bedbug Treatment Procedure:

A comprehensive check of the entire premises is required for bed bug control. Beds, mattresses, pillows, storage cabinets, couches, and linen are the most prevalent items. All cracks and crevices in furniture, bed joints and other fixtures are thoroughly sprayed with insecticidal solution, with specific care paid to mattress beading folds. Due to the resistance of bed bug eggs to pesticide penetration, a second spray treatment is required within 15 days to prevent additional hatchings.
